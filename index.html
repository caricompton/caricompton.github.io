What is the Victim/Witness Assistance Program?

The Victim/Witness Assistance Program is often unheard of until one becomes a victim of a crime. However, it's crucial to know that victims and witnesses are not alone; they have an entire support network dedicated to helping them navigate the complexities of the criminal justice process. Our top priority: advocating for victims' rights and ensuring they have a voice in their pursuit of justice. The first question we ask victims is, "What is their input on the case? So, what rights do victims of crime possess, and what resources are available to them? Will you join us in making a positive impact?
